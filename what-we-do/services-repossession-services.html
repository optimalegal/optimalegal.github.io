Repossession Services

Repossession Services

Fully compliant with the Treating Customers Fairly principles, our repossession services work on either a standalone basis or as part of a wider range of integrated services, giving you the advantage of being able to tailor a property repossession solution which meets your needs.

Our services include:

  • Repossession Litigation
  • Repossession Sales
  • LPA Receivership
  • EPC
  • Title Defects

Working across the UK, our expertise ranges from mortgage rehabilitation and compliance with the pre-action protocol, to borrower insolvency, term expired cases, deceased borrower accounts, service out of jurisdiction and trespass claims to assisted voluntary sales, title validations prior to mortgage portfolio transfers and mortgage shortfall recoveries.

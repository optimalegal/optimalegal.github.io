Debt Purchase

Debt Purchase

We have long-standing relationships with a number of the UK’s largest debt purchasers and have a thorough understanding of the debt purchase market and its various dynamics, including the requirements of the FCA, OFT and CSA.

Working closely with our debt purchase clients our primary objective is to maximise the profitability of each portfolio, subsequently recommending the route, whether legal or otherwise, likely to yield the best commercial return Our expertise extends from pre-legal collection activity, including trace, profiling and field visits, to a straightforward money judgment and recoveries service, or more targeted strategies using specific enforcement methods. We also provide immediate advice and support, should a dispute arise. Working to resolve any dispute quickly and efficiently, ensuring the best commercial terms and outcome.

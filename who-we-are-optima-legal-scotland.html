Optima Legal Scotland

Optima Legal Scotland

We work closely in partnership with Optima Legal Scotland. Their approach and extensive understanding of the Scottish legal process ensures that together we are able to offer the market a wide range of cross border legal services.

The services provided by Optima Legal Scotland include:

  • Debt Litigation (secured and unsecured)
  • Dispute Resolution
  • Consumer Claims
  • Remortgage
  • Residential Conveyancing
  • Professional Negligence
  • Search Services

Optima Legal Scotland Limited is a separate Scottish legal practice, registered in Scotland with number 459802, whose registered office and principal place of business is RWF House, 5 Renfield Street, Glasgow  G2 5EZ. Their VAT number is 186 3619 76.

This practice is authorised and regulated as a multi-national practice by the Law Society of Scotland in accordance with the Professional Practice Rules 2011, which can be viewed at www.lawscot.org.uk 

Optima Legal maintains professional indemnity insurance with Allianz Insurance plc Registered Office, 57 Ladymead, Guildford, Surrey, GU1 1DB under the Law Society of Scotland’s Master Insurance Policy.

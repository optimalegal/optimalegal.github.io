Dispute Resolution & Commercial Litigation

Dispute Resolution & Commercial Litigation

Sometimes disputes arise in relation to the satisfactory performance of a contract, the desired outcome of a project, or the other failures by parties to stick to what they agreed.

Our team of experienced dispute resolution lawyers are on hand to provide immediate advice and support, working to resolve any dispute as quickly and efficiently as possible, ensuring the best commercial terms and outcome.

We currently act for a broad range of corporate clients across many sectors in relation to commercial disputes and actions.  We can provide advice, support and representation in high value and complex disputes before the higher courts as well as providing tailored solutions to your business in order to support the defence of volume actions against businesses.

Our dispute resolution and commercial litigation experience includes:

  • Breach of contract claims arising under service agreements, IT contracts, projects, joint ventures and construction
  • Advice to creditors and debtors in relation to insolvency and administration disputes
  • Disputes arising under consumer credit agreements between business and consumer and on a business to business basis across a range of sectors (such as Financial Services, insurance, utilities)
  • Secured and unsecured recoveries of both commercial and consumer debts, where the value of our client's debt to be recovered through our long-term appointment is in excess of £100,000.

Throughout any dispute, we will passionately represent your best interests until satisfactory conclusion, whether this is by settlement, summary judgment, trial or appeal.

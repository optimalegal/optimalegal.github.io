Fees Assisted Remortgage

Fees Assisted Remortgage

An established and trusted provider of lender instructed ‘fees assisted’ remortgage services

We are proud to work alongside a diverse range of UK mortgage lenders, supporting them with scalable, multi-jurisdictional and innovative remortgage expertise, which together make the remortgage legal process as smooth as possible.

We provide each customer with choice through a best mix of digital and traditional communication channels, which make it easy for customers to keep track and get in touch, including extended opening hours and an online customer case tracking portal.

We also offer a comprehensive range of additional services at preferential rates, so that our clients and customers have affordable access to the legal support they need to ensure that their remortgage stays on track.

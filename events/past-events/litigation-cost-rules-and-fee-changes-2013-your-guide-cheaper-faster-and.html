Litigation cost rules and fee changes 2013: Your guide to cheaper, faster and effective litigation

Litigation cost rules and fee changes 2013: Your guide to cheaper, faster and effective litigation

Tags: 
Debt Litigation

With the changes in litigation court fees and rules coming into effect in England and Wales on 1 April 2013, this workshop allows you to gain insight into these changes, discusses practical ways on how to apply them for the benefit of you and your business and gives the opportunity to ask questions to specialist debt litigation solicitors.

Key topics to be discussed, include:

  • Brief background to the changes and reforms
  • No win, no fee litigation and contingency fees – the future?
  • The end of success fees and ATE insurance?
  • Understanding what’s the best pricing model for your business
  • Taking advantage of the shift in the smalls claims limit
  • Mediation – now a better alternative?
